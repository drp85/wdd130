Music

Music is one of the largest shapers of my life. Listening, composing, playing, performing, and mixing are constants in my life.
Everyone in my family plays at least one instrument. I am a direct descendent of Parley P. Pratt, who wrote several hymns in the LDS hymnal.

Musicianship

I have been a singer my whole life, and I played trombone from 5th grade through high school, including in symphonic, jazz, and marching band ensembles. I also play piano, and can get by on most percussion and brass instruments. I am currently learning Electric and Bass guitar.

Live Sound

My first employment as a sound engineer was for BYU-Idaho Broadcast Productions, where I worked in the I-Center, Hart gymnasium/auditorium, and other venues and setups around the school. I now work in Salt Lake City as a venue/sound technician for the Church of Jesus Christ, as well as a stagehand through the IATSE Local 99 union. I recently worked concerts for Earth, Wind, and Fire; Pierce the Veil, and Kesha.
